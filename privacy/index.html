Privacy Policy

Transparent. Secure. Your data belongs to you.

1. Introduction

At OPTILINE, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it secure.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services and communicate with you. This includes:

  • Personal Info: Name, Email Address, Telegram Username (if provided).
  • Project Data: Information related to your brand or project brief submitted via our forms.
  • Technical Data: Cookies used for website functionality and performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and project briefs.
  • To deliver the services and digital assets you purchased.
  • To improve our website performance and user experience.

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. File Uploads

Files uploaded through our project forms (ZIP files) are stored securely on Google Drive and are accessible only by the OPTILINE team working on your project. We delete project files periodically after delivery.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our business:

  • Google Forms/Drive: For data collection and file storage.
  • PayPal: For secure payment processing. We do not store your credit card details.
  • Cloudflare: For security and spam protection.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
